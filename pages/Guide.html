AceStream Guide

Advantages of AceStream

AceStream uses a peer-to-peer distribution similar to BitTorrent to share live video streams between users. This means that a streamer can set up an AceStream stream, and once users begin to watch the stream, they will help each other to view the stream by uploading the stream to other users.

Thanks to this efficient peer-to-peer distribution model, streamers are able to provide streams to users at high bitrates, and this means a greater quality stream for the user.

The majority of streamers are now using AceStream over SopCast as it offers higher quality, and due to it's distributed nature, better protection from content takedowns.

SopPlayer Manual


1. Introduction

Nowadays SopPlayer, also named SopCast Client, is totally free P2P software to view live TV channels and movies.

This manual takes you through the first Steps of getting SopPlayer and provides the initial pointers to get up and running in no time.

SopPlayer can be run on Windows Vista, Windows XP, Windows 2000 and Linux, but this manual is only for Windows platform.


2. Download and Install


Step 1. Download SopPlayer from http://download.sopcast.com/download/SopCast.zip .

Or you can download it from mirror sites which are listed on page http://www.sopcast.com/download/ .

Step 2. Unzip SopCast.zip with Winzip or Winrar to your hard disc.

Step 3. Go to the directory unzipped and Run Setup-SopCast-verion-num-build-time.exe to install SopCast, the Setup file looks like Setup-SopCast-2.0.4-2007-12-13.exe.

Step 4. Find SopCast in your "Start Menu" and run SopCast.exe.

3. Login

Click the SopCast.exe to start your SopCast experience!

 

[notes] Most channels are available for anonymous users, so if you can't sign in with your account at peak time, please always try with anonymous user.
